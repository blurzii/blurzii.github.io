subjects discribtion

Développement d’application Web

Web development is like being a digital architect, building virtual worlds with just a few clicks and lines of code. Imagine yourself as a tech wizard, creating interactive websites that come to life at the touch of a button. It's a magical journey where you can shape the online landscape, design user experiences, and bring your ideas to life on the web with a sprinkle of creativity and a dash of coding spells! 🌐💻✨ you will enjoy this subject trust me xxxx coef : 2 créd : 4

Réseaux

Networking is like a digital web that connects devices like computers and phones so they can chat and share stuff. It's like a big virtual party where data gets passed around, helping us work together, play games, and surf the web. It's the tech magic that keeps us all connected and makes the digital world go round! 🌐✨ in this subject we learn about how does data paths work and how can we connect devices xd. throw this subject you can become a network and cyber security god. coef : 3 créd : 5

Base de données

Diving into databases is like unlocking a treasure chest of organized information! It's where data gets neatly stored, sorted, and retrieved, making it easy to find what you need. Imagine it as a digital filing cabinet that helps apps, websites, and businesses run smoothly by keeping everything in its right place. So, if you love puzzles and organizing things, databases are your digital playground! 🗄️🔍🌟 in this subjct we learn how data is stored in an officiant and orgnized way xd coef : 3 créd : 5

Système d'exploitation 1

Operating systems are like the superhero sidekicks of your devices! They manage everything behind the scenes, from running apps to juggling tasks, all to make your tech life a breeze. Think of them as the ultimate multitaskers, keeping your computer, phone, or tablet in check so you can focus on being the tech-savvy hero of your digital adventures! 🦸‍♂️🖥️📱 coef : 3 créd : 5

Programmation orientée objets

Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) is like creating a digital universe where everything is a superhero with special powers (objects). These superheroes can talk to each other, team up, and work together to save the day (run your programs). It's a fun and organized way to bring your code to life by thinking of your program as a world full of interactive characters that make coding an epic adventure! 💻🦸‍♂️🌟 coef : 2 créd : 4

Théorie de langage

Language theory is like cracking secret codes in the world of words! It's all about unraveling the mysteries of how languages work, from grammar rules to syntax structures. Imagine yourself as a linguistic detective, decoding the hidden patterns and structures that make languages tick. It's a thrilling journey into the heart of communication, where every word is a clue waiting to be discovered! 🕵️‍♂️🔍📚 coef : 2 créd : 4
